About

The GPA & CGPA Calculator is a modern and intuitive tool designed to help students easily calculate their GPA (Grade Point Average) and CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average). Whether you're managing a single semester or multiple semesters, this tool provides accurate results based on your university's grading system. Our goal is to simplify academic tracking and help students achieve their academic goals.
